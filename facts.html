Space Facts

Discover amazing facts about our universe

Earth

Earth is the only planet known to support life. It has one moon and a protective atmosphere.

The Sun

The Sun contains 99.8% of the solar system's mass and is essential for life on Earth.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its rings made of ice, dust, and rock particles.

Space Travel

Humans have been exploring space since 1961, and missions to Mars are planned for the future.

Did You Know?

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.

A black hole can slow down time due to extreme gravity.

Space is completely silent because there is no air.

Jupiter is so big that 1,300 Earths could fit inside it.

Planet Mars

The Red Planet

Overview

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is known as the Red Planet due to iron oxide on its surface.

Key Facts

  • Distance from Sun: 228 million km
  • Day length: 24.6 hours
  • Moons: 2
  • Temperature: -63°C average
